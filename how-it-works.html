How It Works

ON IN
60 SECONDS.

No app. No pairing. No fuss. Put the headset on and you're already there.

01
🎧

Arrive & Receive

Check in at the door. Pick up your wireless headset — fully charged, freshly cleaned. No app, no account, no pairing required.

02
📻

Pick Your Channel

One button. Three channels. Your headset glows the colour of your channel so you can find your crew across the dancefloor.

03
💃

Dance. Switch. Repeat.

Change channels whenever you want. Take your headset to the bar. Step outside. The music follows you anywhere within 150m.

04
✌️

Drop & Go

Hand the headset back at the collection point when you're done. That's it. The memories are yours to keep.

THE 3 CHANNELS

CHANNEL 1
The headline act. The DJ with the most of the crowd. Curated for the event's primary audience.
CHANNEL 2
The alternative. A different genre, era, or energy. For when you want a change without leaving the room.
CHANNEL 3
The wild card. Always something different. Often surprises people into staying on it all night.
